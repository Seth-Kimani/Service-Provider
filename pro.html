Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to commonly asked questions

What is Pro Xtra, and how does it work with Pro Referral?

Pro Xtra is The Home Depot's loyalty program built for Pros like you to save time, save money, and get rewarded. Click here to learn more about everyday Pro Xtra benefits. Use your Pro Xtra account in-store or online to track your spend. For every $1 you spend with The Home Depot, you earn 2 Pro Referral points that are exchanged for leads. You can view your points in the Pro Referral mobile app or on your Pro Referral account online.

How long does it take to process my documentation?

You can expect your licensing and insurance documentation to be validated within 24 hours. The background check takes about 48 hours.

How many leads will I get with Pro Referral?

How many leads you receive is determined by a variety of factors. The number of services you provide, the number of zip codes you do business in, as well as your performance on Pro Referral all impact how many leads you receive.
