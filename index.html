Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to commonly asked questions

How does Pro Referral match pros to my request?

Pro Referral uses an algorithm to match your request with qualified local pros. We'll refer the top pros in your area who meet the criteria of your project, so you can be sure you're connecting with relevant, local pros!

Do you share my personal information with my selected pros?

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. We’ll only share necessary personal information to the pros you match with so that they may contact you regarding your project. This includes your phone number and email address but does not include your home address.

I've submitted a request. What happens next?

Once you have submitted a request, we'll send you an email confirmation and notify your pros. The pros will then reach out to you directly within 48 business hours to discuss your job and needs.After connecting with all the pros, take your time comparing options, pricing estimates, and other project details (this includes verifying that your pro's licensing is valid and adequate for your project). You'll then select the best pro for your project and budget.
